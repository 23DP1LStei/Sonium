About Sonium

Sonium is a music rating application designed to bring music lovers together through a shared experience of discovery and evaluation. The platform allows users to register, search for music albums, and rate them on a scale from 1 to 10. In addition to providing a personal rating experience, Sonium offers the ability to view and filter previously rated albums and see how others in the local user community have rated the same albums. Whether you're an avid music collector, a casual listener, or someone looking to explore new sounds through the eyes of others, Sonium provides a simple and intuitive way to keep track of your musical journey.

User Interface Overview

Sonium's interface is designed to be clean, intuitive, and accessible across devices. It includes:

• A responsive navigation bar

• Search functionality for quick album discovery

• A simple rating interface

• Filtering and sorting of rated albums

• A dashboard for managing user interactions


New users can easily register to create a personal account. This enables the app to track user-specific ratings and preferences, and allows access to community-based features like local rating comparisons.

User Manual

Welcome to the Sonium user manual! This guide will walk you through all the features and functionality of our music rating application, helping you get the most out of your experience.

1Getting Started

Registration

When you start Sonium for the first time, you'll be prompted to enter your username:

  1. Launch the application
  2. At the prompt "Enter your name:", type your desired username
  3. Press Enter to continue

If you've used Sonium before, the system will recognize your username and load your existing profile. If you're new, it will create a profile for you automatically.

Tip: Choose a memorable username that represents your music personality!

2Navigating the Main Menu

After registering, you'll see the main menu with four options:

Option Description
1. Find an album Search for music albums to rate or mark as listened
2. My albums View and manage albums you've rated or marked as listened
3. Top 20 albums by local users See albums highly rated by the Sonium community
4. Quit Exit the application

To select an option, simply type the corresponding number (1-4) and press Enter.

3Finding and Rating Albums

Searching for Albums

To find an album:

  1. Select option "1" from the main menu
  2. Enter part or all of the album title when prompted
  3. The system will display a list of matching albums
  4. Choose an album by entering its corresponding number

Album Actions

Once you've selected an album, you'll see three options:

  1. Mark as listened - Records that you've listened to this album
  2. Rate an album - Give the album a rating from 1 to 5
  3. View average rating - See the average rating from all Sonium users
Note: Ratings must be between 1 and 5, where 1 is the lowest and 5 is the highest.

4Managing Your Albums

To view and manage your album collection:

  1. Select option "2" from the main menu
  2. Choose a sorting option for your albums:
    • Date added (newest first)
    • Date added (oldest first)
    • Rating (highest first)
    • Rating (lowest first)
  3. Your albums will be displayed in a table showing the title, artist, rating, and date added

Deleting Albums from Your List

To remove an album from your collection:

  1. After viewing your album list, select option "1" to delete an album
  2. Enter the number of the album you wish to delete
  3. Confirm the deletion by typing "y" or "yes" when prompted
Tip: The color-coded display makes it easy to scan through your collection. Albums are displayed in alternating cyan and green colors for better readability.

5Exploring Top Rated Albums

Sonium allows you to discover popular albums rated by other users:

  1. Select option "3" from the main menu
  2. Choose a decade filter:
    • All time
    • 1960s
    • 1970s
    • 1980s
    • 1990s
    • 2000s and later
  3. The system will display the top 20 albums for your selected time period, showing title, artist, year, and average rating
  4. Press Enter to return to the main menu when you're done browsing
Tip: This is a great way to discover new music based on what other Sonium users enjoy!

6Exiting the Application

To safely exit Sonium:

  1. Select option "4" from the main menu
  2. The application will display a goodbye message and close
Important: All your ratings and listened albums are automatically saved, so you can always pick up where you left off next time.

Keyboard Shortcuts and Tips

💡
Navigate efficiently - Always have the number of your choice ready to make selections quickly.
💡
Search strategically - When searching for albums, using shorter keywords can yield more results.
💡
Rating system - The rating scale is 1-5, where 1 is the lowest and 5 is the highest rating.
💡
Listening vs. Rating - You can mark albums as "listened" without rating them if you're not ready to provide a score.

Troubleshooting

⚠️
Problem: Application doesn't start
Solution: Ensure Java is properly installed on your system and that you're running the command from the correct directory.
⚠️
Problem: No albums found when searching
Solution: Try using a more general keyword or check for typos in your search term.
⚠️
Problem: Rating not accepted
Solution: Ensure you're entering a number between 1 and 5.
